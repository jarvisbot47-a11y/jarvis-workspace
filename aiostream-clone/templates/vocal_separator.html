💡 About Demucs Stem Separation
Demucs (by Meta Research) uses deep learning to separate audio into 4 stems: Vocals, Drums, Bass, and Other (instruments/melody).

Models: htdemucs offers the best quality. htdemucs_ft is finer-tuned but slower. hdemucs is the hybrid model. demucs is the original.
Processing time is approximately 1–2× the audio length on CPU.
