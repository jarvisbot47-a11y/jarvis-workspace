How to Connect a Platform

Instagram

  1. Click "Add Profile" → select Instagram
  2. Click "Open" to launch browser
  3. Log in to Instagram manually
  4. Browser stays logged in (cookies saved)
  5. Return here and click "Check" to confirm

TikTok

  1. Add TikTok profile
  2. Open browser and log in at tiktok.com
  3. Cookies are auto-saved
  4. Use Post Scheduler to queue videos

YouTube

  1. Add YouTube profile
  2. Open and log in at youtube.com
  3. Best for upload workflow via YouTube Studio

Facebook

  1. Add Facebook profile
  2. Open browser and log in
  3. Schedule posts for your Page or Profile
