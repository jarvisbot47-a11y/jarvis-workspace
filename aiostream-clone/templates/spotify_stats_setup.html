How it works

Spotify for Artists (artist-analytics.spotify.com) shows real streaming data — monthly listeners, streams, followers, playlist placements, and demographics.

To access real data: Any Spotify Premium account that has claimed the artist page can log in. The scraper uses Playwright to automate the browser and read your real stats.

No Premium? Stats will fall back to data logged by the streaming automation (real stream counts from your account pool).
