# SECTION 2: DIGITAL DISTRIBUTION & PLATFORM STRATEGY

## Where to Focus (Priority Order)

### 1. Spotify (HIGHEST PRIORITY)
Spotify remains the #1 streaming platform for hip hop discovery. Everything flows from Spotify presence.

**Playlist Strategy:**
- **Submit to Spotify for Artists Early Access** — use the "Upcoming" release tool 7 days before release
- **Target 3 types of playlists:**
  - **Editorial playlists** (Rap Caviar, Front Page, New Music Friday) — pitch via Spotify for Artists
  - **Independent curators** — use tools like **Groover**, **SubmitHub**, or **PlaylistPush** to pitch directly to curators with follow counts 500-5,000 (higher conversion rate than massive playlists)
  - **Your own fan playlist** — create and share a "Memento Mori Official Playlist" on Spotify linking all three albums + features
- **Pre-save campaigns** — use **Linkfire** or **Spotify Pre-Save** (via DistroKid/TuneCore) to drive pre-release hype
- **Spotify Canvas** — upload 3-8 second looping video for every track. Research shows higher engagement.

**Spotify for Artists Tips:**
- Claim your profile fully (artist bio, photo, verified)
- Use the "Discovery Mode" boost on your best-performing tracks
- Analyze your Audience data monthly — know which cities/age groups/demographics are listening

### 2. TikTok (NON-NEGOTIABLE DISCOVERY ENGINE)
TikTok is still where songs go viral in 2026. It is the most democratic platform for independent artists.

**Content Types That Work for Rap:**
- **Snippet drops** — 15-30 second snippets of your hardest verse. Let fans react.
- **"Part That Made Me Stop Recording"** — post the moment in a song that hits different
- **Freestyle/reaction videos** — you freestyling over a beat or reacting to your own music
- **Storytime** — talk about the inspiration behind a song or feature
- **Feature collabs** — post clips of you and a feature working together in the studio
- **Memento Mori lore** — what does the philosophy mean to you? Post about it.
- **Lyric breakdowns** — explain bars that have deeper meaning
- **Trend jacking** — monitor trending sounds and adapt them to your style (always authentic)

**Hashtags to Use:**
`#HipHop #Rap #NewMusic #IndieArtist #MystikSingh #MementoMori #UndergroundHipHop`

**TikTok Best Practices:**
- Post 1-2x daily minimum during active campaigns
- Use local hashtags for regional reach alongside national ones
- Always include a call-to-action: "Link in bio," "Comment your take," "Duet this"
- Respond to every comment in the first hour of posting

### 3. Instagram (BRAND BUILDING + REELS)
Instagram Reels = discovery. Instagram Stories = connection. Instagram Grid = credibility.

**Content Calendar for IG:**
- Reels: 4-5x per week (short-form, TikTok-style content adapted for IG audience)
- Stories: Daily (behind-the-scenes, polls, Q&As, countdowns)
- Grid: Maintain aesthetic consistency — this is your digital storefront

**Reels Specific Tactics:**
- Post the same content from TikTok (repurpose!) but optimize for IG's audience
- Use Instagram's "Add to Music" feature when posting so your track is discoverable on-platform
- Collaborate with micro-influencers (5K-50K followers) in the hip hop space for Reels takeovers

### 4. YouTube (LONG-FORM + DISCOVERY)
YouTube Shorts = TikTok-level discovery. Long-form YouTube = legacy content.

**Shorts Strategy:**
- Post every TikTok/Reels video as a YouTube Short as well
- Shorts that perform well can drive subscribers to your main channel
- Target: 90+ Shorts by end of Phase 2

**Long-Form Content to Create:**
- **Album listening parties** (live stream or pre-recorded)
- **Studio session footage** — raw, unfiltered
- **"The Memento Mori Story"** — documentary-style video about the trilogy's creation
- **Lyric videos** for standout tracks (use AI tools — see Section 4)
- **Music videos** for singles (even low-budget AI-assisted ones beat no video)

### 5. Apple Music + Amazon Music + Tidal
Don't sleep on these. Submit directly via their artist platforms:
- **Apple Music for Artists** — claim profile, submit for editorial consideration
- **Amazon Music for Artists** — often overlooked, easier editorial placement
- **Tidal Artist** — Tidal pays higher per-stream rates; worth a focused push

# SECTION 5: RELEASE STRATEGY — THE WATERFALL METHOD

## Single Release Waterfall (8 Weeks Per Single)

**Week 1-2: Seed the Snippet**
- Post 15-second snippet on TikTok/Reels
- Watch for organic viral signal
- If a snippet starts to spread, lean into it hard

**Week 3: Pre-Save Launch**
- Launch pre-save campaign via DistroKid/TuneCore + Linkfire
- Email existing list with pre-save link
- Post "coming Friday" content

**Week 4: THE DROP**
- Release everywhere simultaneously
- Full music video drops on YouTube
- Send to all playlist curators the morning of release
- Maximum posting frequency (2-3 posts that day across all platforms)

**Week 5-6: Ride the Wave**
- Post daily content related to the single for 2 weeks
- Engage with every comment, duet, and reaction
- Pitch to editorial playlists (Spotify, Apple Music)

**Week 7-8: Convert to Catalog**
- Add single to Memento Mori playlist
- Create lyric video
- Begin seeding the next single's snippet

# SECTION 6: FEATURE PLACEMENT TACTICS

Your three albums already have features — now activate them:

### For Existing Features:
1. **Tag everything** — every social post about featured tracks should tag the featured artist
2. **Create "Feature Supercuts"** — compile all your featured artists into one highlight reel
3. **Ask features to cross-post** — reach out to each featured artist and ask them to share the collab on their platforms (most will if you ask genuinely)
4. **Create feature-specific content** — clips, quotes, stories centered on each collaboration

### For Future Features:
- When choosing features, prioritize artists who:
  - Have active, engaged fanbases (even if smaller)
  - Post consistently on social media
  - Are active collaborators themselves
  - Share your thematic overlap (not just anyone who will say yes)
- **Structure features as events** — announce features with a 48-hour countdown, create hype

# SECTION 7: EMAIL MARKETING — THE MONEY CHANNEL

Email is the one channel you **own**. Social platforms can ban or shadowban you. Algorithm changes can kill your reach overnight. But your email list is yours forever.

**How to Build It:**
1. **Lead magnet:** Offer exclusive content in exchange for email sign-up (e.g., "Get the Memento Mori Vol. 4 demo + unreleased stems — exclusive to email list")
2. **Link in every bio** — use a service like **Linktree** or **Link in Bio** to aggregate your links
3. **Link in every social post** — "Join the list: link in bio"
4. **Contest/giveaway** — "Sign up for a chance to win a studio session with me"

**What to Send:**
- **New release announcements** (never more promotional than this)
- **Exclusive demos or unreleased tracks**
- **Early access to tickets/merch**
- **Behind-the-scenes stories** (personal, not generic)
- **Personal notes** — occasional "I was thinking about you today..." type email from you directly

**Email Platform:** Use **Mailchimp** (free up to 500 contacts) or **ConvertKit** (better for creators, ~$9/mo)

# SECTION 8: PLAYLIST PITCHING — THE SYSTEMATIC APPROACH

Playlist placement is a volume game combined with smart targeting.

### Playlist Types to Target:
1. **Editorial (Spotify/Apple Music)** — Submit via for Artists dashboard, 7+ days before release
2. **Independent Curators (500-5K followers)** — Higher acceptance rate. Find them via Groover, SubmitHub, or by manually searching Spotify for curators in your sub-genre
3. **User-Generated Playlists** — Collaborative playlists your fans create. Amplify the best ones.

### The Pitch Email Template:
```
Subject: [Artist Name] — [Track Title] | [Genre] | Would love your consideration

Hey [Curator Name],

I'm [Artist Name], an independent [genre] artist. I just released "[Track Title]" 
and thought it might be a fit for your [Playlist Name].

The track blends [2-3 genre descriptors] with a focus on [mood/theme]. 
Fans of [2 reference artists] have been resonating with it.

[1-2 sentence story behind the song — makes it human]

I noticed your playlist features [something specific about their taste] 
and I'd love to be part of that if it's a fit.

Spotify link: [link]
Apple Music: [link]

Thanks for your time either way — keep doing what you're doing with [playlist name].

[Artist Name]
[Social links]
```
